Manage correctly your European customers with their own tax laws about digital goods.

== Description ==

From January 1st 2015, the European Union has put into effect the new rules about the taxation on the purchase of all digital goods for European citizens.
This makes necessary to apply the right taxes for every purchaser of your e-commerce.
Therefore, whether your users are direct consumers or businesses, YITH WooCommerce EU VAT allows your customers to be identified by their country, applying automatically the expected taxes, and summarizing all the amounts you will have to pay monthly. An efficient and automatic tool to respect the new legislation.

= Main features =

* Taxes are applied based on the customer location.
* Pre built EU Tax rates list.
* Choose the taxes to pay with a MOSS report filtered by nation and time
* Choose to prevent purchases from EU customers.
* In case of you want to prevent purchases from EU customers, choose to show customized messages on cart and on checkout page.
* Store automatically the EU taxes paid by the customer.
* Consult quickly a direct report about the taxes to pay by nation and period of time.

For a more detailed list of options and features of the plugin, please look at the [official documentation](http://yithemes.com/docs-plugins/yith-woocommerce-eu-vat/ "Yith WooCommerce EU VAT official documentation").

Discover all the features of the plugin and install it in your theme: the result will be extremely satisfying.

== Installation ==
Important: First of all, you have to download and activate WooCommerce plugin, which is mandatory for YITH WooCommerce EU VAT to be working.

1. Download and unzip the downloaded file.
2. Upload the plugin folder into the `wp-content/plugins/` directory of your WordPress site.
3. Activate `YITH WooCommerce EU VAT` from "Plugins" page.

= Configuration =

YITH WooCommerce EU VAT will add a new tab called "EU VAT" in "YIT Plugins" menu item. There, you will find all Yithemes plugins with quick access to plugin setting page.

== Screenshots ==

1. In order to deal with EU VAT laws, the shop owner can decide to deny purchases from EU customers.
2. Customize the message to show to EU customers on checkout, when the plugin is to deny purchases from EU customers.

6. Import all EU VAT tax rates into WooCommerce "standard" rates class.
7. Select from the tax rate list, which tax should be taken in account when dealing with EU VAT laws.
8. Print a report with details about VAT taxes paid by EU customers.
